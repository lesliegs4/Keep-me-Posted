# Keep-me-Posted

## App Definition Statement:
Keep me Posted is meant to help user’s keep track of their travel adventures or simply their favorite locations. It is meant to simulate having a diary where you can journal about your experiences at a certain place, create a postcard to send to your friends and family, and pin the different places you’ve visited. The objective is to give people a space to document their experiences and share them with their loved ones. It is meant for travelers and writers alike, just giving people a space to tell the stories behind their photos.

## Key features:
Postcard Generator
Map with Saved Location Pins
Journaling feature

## User Demographics: 
Meant for travelers and writers of any age. Mainly focused on targeting 15-40 years old, but obviously, there’s no actual limit.

## Frameworks Used:
1. Sign in with Google: for login and signup purposes
2. Firebase: to keep track of users login information and storage for their profile elements. For example, journal entries, contacts, 
3. Apple MapKit, native framework for providing map interface
4. Contacts list: for the postcard sending and visible on the profile page
